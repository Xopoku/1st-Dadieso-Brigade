USER GUIDE

Any user of this application must read the following information carefully and obey them.
  1. In the Home-Page is red button, Click on it to read more on the history of the Boys' and Girls' Brigade.

  2. You can flip right or click on the 3 lines on top of the App menu bar to open the navigation drawer. After opening the drawer, select any item of your choice to open it.

  3. On the drawer item is News and Events. This is the place where you can find our daily news, upcoming programs and other events. NOTE: The Annual Activities section all uses Network so make sure to on your WIFI or MOBILE DATA.

  4. All Band Members are to click on the Band Unit item and sign In to access and download Musical Scores and others.

  5. Members Can also get access to our Videos by clicking on the Video Library Item.

  6. The FB Library will direct you to our facebook page. For the first time make sure you sign In with facebook using your facebook account details.

  7. When you click on Email item under the Contact Us Section, make sure to select you preferred Email App and then click on Always.

  8. Moreover, in the Home-page of the app there are some moving images(Sliders). Click of the type that says CLICK FOR MORE.
